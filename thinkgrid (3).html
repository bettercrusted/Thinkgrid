Help

A few things worth knowing.

Double-click empty canvas to drop a note. Select a note to style it from the panel at the bottom. Press L to link two notes, H for the hand tool, D for drawing mode. Plain scroll pans the canvas; hold Ctrl/Cmd while scrolling (or pinch on a trackpad) to zoom toward your cursor.

The Assistant panel offers on-device heuristics — summarizing, clustering and rewriting text using simple local rules, not a connected AI model. Everything autosaves to this browser.
